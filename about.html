Hello! I'm Carolyn Bahar. I'm a designer and illustrator.

To be concise, I say I'm a designer and illustrator, but there's more to it. I'm a maker, a storyteller, a thinker in all sorts of mediums.

My purpose and passion is to make things for people. Things that not only are usable and useful but also evoke feelings -- curiosity, inspiration, sentimentality... You get the idea. Right now, I'd say I'm more of a jack of all trades rather than a master at one, but I think that's how it should be right now while I'm exploring and trying to learn as much as possible.

I'm currently wrapping up my final semester at UNC-Chapel Hill where I'm studying graphic design and UX design in the Media and Journalism program, as well as printmaking in the Fine Art program. I'm also a freelance designer for Lucky Peach every now and then and am a design intern at Splinter Group. I've previously interned for Lucky Peach and The Knot.

Aside from design, I do other things! I've started going bouldering, which is really fun. I'm really into baking and cooking. Lately, I've been trying stuff from Kinfolk and Ottolenghi's cookbook. I am very into the idea of urban farming and have kind of been obsessing and reading and dreaming about it a lot, so wherever I move I want to grow as much as possible like herbs, tea, and whatnot. I love traveling, exploring different places, and of course try new foods in different cultures. I don't know what else to say. I'm nice. Let's chat.
