SuckedIn.com

In my UX design class during Fall 2015, our client was a UNC-Chapel Hill tobacco research center. The site that they are in the process of making, SuckedIn.com, is meant to be to raise awareness of OTP, other tobacco products. I interviewed people about their behaviors regarding how they navigate online and about OTP. Afterwards, I designed wireframes based on the information I got. I then tested those bare bones wireframes. From there, I made changes and made the design panels. It was very valuable to learn about user testing for a client.
