Print Ain't Dead / Click Here to Subscribe

This print is speaking to both the digital and analog work that I do as a designer. I love designing for both print and digital. But ultimately, there is this huge shift to the internet. No matter how loud we scream that print isn't dead, it's impossible to stay alive without web presence. This print represents my mindset of the time about the print industries that I have so much love for.
