Finish Line Logo

While I was a designer at the Carolina Union, I designed multiple logo options for The Finish Line Project, a program to help first-generation undergraduate students. The client requested that the logo be Carolina blue and have imagery related to graduation. These are six concepts, and I believe they ended up going with a good ol' combination of mine in the top left corner and another designer's logo who was also working on it.
