Vintage Kicks Are In

For the intermediate interactive coding class in Spring 2015, I had to code a slider and write and design the content on the slider. I wanted it to be similar to what a lot of publications have online -- a slider with a list of their favorite salad dressing recipes or a list of cool winter fashion trends. You get the idea. So I made mine about sneakers that were making a comeback, and I illustrated all of them of course. You can also rate the sneakers if you have like.
