Fantastic Mr. Fox: Penguin Book

I designed this traditional Penguin book for Fantastic Mr. Fox. I took certain creative liberties when applying the Penguin style. It was a great learning experience learning not only how to design a book but also how to physically put a book together. I binded mine using perfect binding method. It was a real trial and error process, but I'm proud of the end result and that I pushed myself to do something different.
