The Knot & Macy's Wedding Registry Guide

During Summer 2014, I was the art/design intern The Knot, which is the publication of XO Group Inc. I had the opportunity to design these spreads for The Knot and Macy's Wedding Registry Guide. The guide is no longer online, but it did include hyperlinks to all the items. These were designed under the art direction of Nicolette Berthelot and Meghan Corrigan.
